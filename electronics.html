Nintendo Switch Lite

The Nintendo Switch Lite is the cheapest of all three of our Nintendo Switch models. It is completely hand held and is easy to use anywhere. It is compact and lightweight and has the same game compatability as all other nintendo switch models It comes in five unique colors: gray, turquoise, yellow, coral, and blue.

Nintendo Switch

The Nintendo Switch is mean't to be played ANYWHERE. With a TV mode, tabletop mode, and handheld mode it can be enjoyed anywhere. It comes with two detachable Joy-Con controllers. With the IR motion camera a whole new catologue of games is available. Multiplayer can be enjoyed in local co-op, local wireless, or online with a Nintendo Switch Online subscription.

Nintendo Switch OLED Model

The OLEDmodel has all the same features as the original Nintendo Switch but has a 7-inch OLED screen. It comes with a wider adjustable stand and 64GB of internal storage and has enhanced audio. The OLED screen allows for vivid colors and clearer graphics.
