Games

Super Mario Odyssey

In super Mario odyssey you get to play as Mario and his new ally Cappy as you explore 17 unique kingdoms with the final goal being to rescue Princess Peach. Because of Cappy Mario has brand new, never before seen moves. You get to use object, enemies, and animals to progress further through the story.

Animal Crossing New Horizons

In Animal Crossing: New Horizons you get to create your own island and build it from the ground up. You get to discover the nature around you. You can customize your character, home, decorations, and the landscaping of the island. The time of day and seasons even match real life time according to your time zone.

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

In Breath of the Wild you get to travel across fields, forests, and mountains to discover the Hyrule kingdom in all its glory. Comparted to other Legend of Zelda games this one is freer and more open than ever before. Forgot everything you know about the Legend of Zelda and step into this new world.
